About Me

Hi, I'm Vinay, a dedicated final-year B.Tech student in Computer Science and Engineering (AIML) at DRK Institute of Science and Technology, affiliated with JNTUH, Hyderabad. I’m passionate about building intelligent systems and scalable full-stack applications. My core interests lie in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and modern web & mobile development. I enjoy transforming ideas into practical, real-world solutions through clean architecture and efficient design. One of my key projects is an AI-based Plagiarism Detection System, where I integrated machine learning models and modern web technologies to detect copied and AI-generated content effectively. Currently, I’m developing an AI-powered Workout App using Flutter (Dart) for the frontend and PHP with MySQL for the backend. This application focuses on personalized workout generation, user progress tracking, and intelligent fitness recommendations powered by AI concepts. I have hands-on experience in Deep Learning, Computer Vision, FastAPI, React, API integration, and database management. I am continuously learning, building, and improving — aiming to create impactful AI-driven solutions while growing both technically and personally.
